Process

  1. Discovery: clarify purpose, audience, and success metrics.
  2. Wireframe & Content: structure pages and collect assets.
  3. Build: implement clean, semantic code with reusable classes.
  4. Test: cross browser checks, mobile responsiveness, accessibility.
  5. Optimize & Launch: performance tweaks, SEO basics, deployment.

Outcomes

Clients get a site that loads quickly, looks great on any device, and is easy to update. By focusing on performance and user experience, the result is higher engagement, clearer messaging, and better conversion from visitors.
